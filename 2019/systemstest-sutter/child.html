Sutter Health snapshot

A look at information from the system’s cost reports from 2012 to 2017

  • Established: Created in 1981, with affiliated hospitals dating back to the 1800s.
  • Locations: Mainly Northern California with headquarters in Sacramento
  • Total employee and contractor FTEs: 24,719 (in 2017)
  • Acute inpatient discharges: 159,187 (in 2017)

Range of total profit(loss) margins

High, low and median margins are calculated using cost reports from Modern Healthcare Metrics.

  • The green bar shows the highest margin out of the cost reports associated with the system each year.
  • The orange bar shows the lowest.
  • The red line at the point the two bars meet shows the median margin.
  • The blue line shows total profit(loss) margin for the entire system from Modern Healthcare databases.

Example: In 2012, there were 23 cost reports covering Short-Term hospitals in the system. The lowest reported margin was -6.2%, the highest was 55.42%. Median total margin for the 23 reports was 6.86%. Total profit(loss) margin for the entire system in 2012 was 8.07%.
