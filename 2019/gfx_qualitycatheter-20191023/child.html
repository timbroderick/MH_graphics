Reducing harm

The Children’s Hospitals’ Solutions for Patient Safety network advocates for best practices to prevent injuries and reduce costs. For one measure, SPS reports a decline of 1.3 infections per 1,000 catheter line days since January 2011, even while the number of participating hospitals increased.
